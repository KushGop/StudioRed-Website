Parkdale Park


Location: Ottawa ON. / Completed 2011
Client: City of Ottawa

“ There is fun to be done! There are points to be scored. There are games to be won. And the magical things you can do with that ball will make you the winningest winner of all.” Dr. Seuss

Kaja Cerveny was the project landscape architect and lead on the Parkdale Park renovation project while with Douglas & Ruhland Landscape Architects. His collaboration with subconsultant Colizza Bruni Architecture who were responsible for the community field house renovation and new park performance stage resulted in a revitalized and fun community space enjoyed by all ages. The project won the City of Ottawa Urban design Award of Excellence for Public Places and Civic Spaces.
